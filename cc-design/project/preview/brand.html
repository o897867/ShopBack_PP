Voice · do & don't

Voice · DO

  • Lead with Chinese, let English follow in sentence case
  • End CTAs with a literal arrow: 立即开户 →
  • Say "we" (我们), never "I"
  • Keep disclaimers dry and occasionally dry-witted on Fortune only
  • Use mono type for any number, timestamp, or source

Voice · DON'T

  • No exclamation marks anywhere
  • No Title Case English
  • No emoji in editorial or marketing copy
  • No "unlock", "revolutionary", "game-changing" language
  • No emoji icons — use Lucide at stroke 1.5 instead
